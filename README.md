# The Economics of Artificial Intelligence: A Comprehensive Analysis

**Author:** Andrea Trenti  
**Date:** February 2026

## Overview

Comprehensive economic analysis of AI covering productivity impacts, infrastructure investment, labor markets, adoption patterns, regulation, and sustainability constraints.

## Key Findings

- **Productivity:** 1.5-3.0% GDP increase by 2035, 0.7-1.5% TFP gains over 10 years
- **Investment:** $602B hyperscaler capex in 2026 (75% AI-specific)
- **Market Size:** $244B (2025) → $827B-$1.2T (2030) at 27-33% CAGR
- **Labor:** 85M jobs displaced, 97M created (net +12M globally)
- **Adoption:** 88% organizations use AI, 71% use GenAI
- **Energy:** 183 TWh (2024) → 426 TWh (2030), ~12% of US electricity
